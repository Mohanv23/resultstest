Results

Your assessment results will appear below after they have been marked and approved by the SQE assessment board. This can take between 6-10 weeks for SQE1 and between 14-18 weeks for SQE2. Results dates are published on the website as they are confirmed. We will send you an email when your results are available and have been posted onto your Candidate Account.

You can visit this page at any time to see your approved results. You can also save a PDF of your results for your personal records and printing.

Below your results are instructions on your next steps after passing all assessments. There is also information about what to do if you want to appeal your result, or if you have failed your assessment.

Booking your next assessment or resit

Be aware of your attempt number and deadline to pass – You are allowed 3 attempts at each assessment and 6 years to pass all SQE assessments.

To book your next assessment go to: Assessment bookings

How the pass mark is set

For further information about marking and how the pass mark is set please go to the Marking and Standard Setting Policy.

Appeals

For information about requests for clarification, clerical checks and appeals against assessment board decisions please go to the Assessment Regulations and the Appeals Policy.

Admission to the Roll

Once you have passed all the SQE assessments that you are required to sit, and satisfied the other requirements for admission, you can apply to the SRA for admission to the roll of solicitors of England and Wales.

Apprentice candidates

The information shown here is a record of your SQE results only, and is not a formal apprenticeship certificate. If you have passed SQE2 (the end point assessment), we will apply to the ESFA on your behalf for an end of apprenticeship certificate (if you have provided your consent to us to do this). Only the ESFA can formally issue end of apprenticeship certificates.

Next steps

Booking your next assessment

Before booking your next assessment or resit you need to:

  • Update your answers to the monitoring and maximising diversity survey Opens new window
  • Apply for an exemption Opens new window if applicable
  • Confirm if you require reasonable adjustments Opens new window and request them if need be

Then you can book your next assessment or resit by going to: Assessment bookings Opens new window

Got an exemption to SQE2

If you have already been granted an SQE2 exemption, please tell us in your SQE account. Find out how to do this by going to Exemptions and using SQE2 as an equivalence

Completed all assessments

Having passed (or gained exemptions to) the SQE assessments that you are required to complete, you can now request your results to be added to mySRA
